About

Flown by a pilot,
built for your project

TriPilot is operated by Cameron Weatherspoon — a commercially-rated pilot and FAA Part 107 certified drone operator based in Murfreesboro, TN. With a B.S. in Aerospace from Middle Tennessee State University, Cameron brings real aviation discipline to every flight: thorough planning, safe execution, and professional results.

Every project is planned with airspace awareness, weather monitoring, and client communication — so you get the shot you need, delivered on time.

  • FAA Part 107 Remote Pilot Certificate
